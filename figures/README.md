# Figures

##PCA and volcano plots
###PCA Plot
PCA showed a clear separation between normal and disease-associated samples. Adenoma and carcinoma samples partially overlapped, supporting a progression pattern from normal tissue to adenoma and carcinoma.

###Volcano Plots
Volcano plots identified significantly upregulated and downregulated genes across all comparisons.
- Cancer vs Normal showed the strongest transcriptomic differences.
- Adenoma vs Normal revealed early molecular changes.
- Adenoma vs Cancer showed fewer extreme changes, indicating partial similarity between the two disease states.

---

##Heatmap
The heatmap of top differentially expressed genes demonstrated distinct expression patterns between Normal, Adenoma, and Cancer (Carcionoma) samples. Normal tissue formed a clearly separate expression cluster.

---

##GO enrichment dotplots
GO analysis highlighted biological processes related to:
- extracellular matrix remodeling,
- hypoxia response,
- tissue organization,
- fibroblast proliferation,
- metabolic and inflammatory processes.

---

##KEGG pathway analysis
KEGG enrichment identified pathways associated with:
- inflammatory signaling,
- cytokine interactions,
- NF-kappa B signaling,
- metabolic alterations,
- tissue remodeling and cancer progression.
  
---

##Enrichment network plots
The enrichment network plot showed clusters of interconnected biological processes involved in inflammation, extracellular matrix remodeling, hypoxia response, and tumor progression.
